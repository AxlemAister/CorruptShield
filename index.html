Welcome

CorruptShield is an AI-powered initiative to promote transparency, fight corruption, and improve governance in the Philippines. By combining open data, blockchain technology, and community participation, we aim to protect public funds and ensure infrastructure projects truly benefit the people.

Problem Statement

The Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH) has been repeatedly cited for corruption scandals. Alleged issues include:

  • Ghost projects and non-existent infrastructure
  • Overpriced and substandard materials
  • Collusion in bidding and contract rigging
  • Unregulated bicameral alterations of project funding
  • Uncategorized and generic “flood control” allocations
  • Misuse of the Engineering Administrative Overhead (EAO) fund
  • Rock netting and cat’s eye reflector controversies

According to reports, as much as ₱4.5 billion has been lost to anomalous infrastructure projects in recent years. Nationwide, alleged misuse and corruption in DPWH projects are estimated to reach tens of billions of pesos annually — funds that could have built resilient roads, effective flood control systems, and safe schools.

Our Solution

CorruptShield introduces a comprehensive toolset for transparency and accountability:

  • AI Bid Vetting: Detects collusion and red flags in procurement.
  • Blockchain Ledger: Tracks project budgets, timelines, and contracts immutably.
  • LIDAR & Remote Sensing: Validates whether infrastructure projects are real and compliant.
  • Agentic AI Oversight: Continuously monitors projects for anomalies.
  • AI Chatbot: Provides citizens with real-time project updates and budget tracking.
  • International Standards: Ensures DPWH projects align with global best practices.
  • Transparent Dashboard: Accessible by DPWH, COA, LGUs, and the public.
  • M4GG (Mayors for Good Governance): Mobilizes local leaders to monitor projects on the ground.

By combining these technologies, CorruptShield makes corruption harder to hide, empowering communities and ensuring public funds are spent properly.
