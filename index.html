Welcome

CorruptShield is an AI-powered initiative to promote transparency, fight corruption, and improve governance in the Philippines. By combining open data, blockchain technology, and community participation, we aim to protect public funds and ensure infrastructure projects truly benefit the people.

Problem Statement

The Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH) is among the most corruption-prone agencies in the Philippines. Billions of pesos are lost annually to ghost projects, overpriced contracts, substandard construction, and lack of accountability. Our Solution

CorruptShield introduces a Transparent Dashboard accessible to the public, DPWH, and the Commission on Audit (COA). By using AI to analyze procurement patterns and detect anomalies, and blockchain to securely track budgets and contracts, we make corruption harder to hide. We also launch the M4GG (Mayors for Good Governance) initiative to empower local leaders to monitor projects at the community level.
