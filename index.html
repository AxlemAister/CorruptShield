Welcome

CorruptShield is an AI-powered initiative built to expose corruption in infrastructure projects and ensure that public funds deliver real value for Filipino communities.

Problem Statement

Recent official data reveals staggering corruption in Philippine flood control projects:

  • Since 2022, **₱545 billion** was allocated for flood control—with many projects found to be substandard, undocumented, or entirely fictitious. 0
  • The Department of Finance estimates ghost projects alone cost the economy between **₱42 billion–₱118.5 billion** from 2023 to 2025. That's equivalent to 95,000–266,000 lost jobs. 1
  • Over the past 15 years (2011–2025), more than **₱1.9 trillion** was allocated to flood control, with possibly **half (₱1 trillion)** lost to corruption. 2

These figures represent not just lost funds, but broken lives, unsafe infrastructure, and unprepared communities. Corruption dressed as public works has failed to deliver—and all too often, there's no one held accountable.

Our Solution

CorruptShield offers a comprehensive defense against corruption with:

  • AI Bid Vetting: Detects irregularities and red flags in contract proposals.
  • Blockchain Tracking: Ensures immutability in budget flows, contracts, and project milestones.
  • LIDAR & Geospatial Verification: Confirms whether claimed infrastructures truly exist.
  • Agentic AI Oversight: Provides real-time monitoring and anomaly alerts.
  • Citizen Chatbot Interface: Empowers communities to check project status and report issues.
  • International Best Practices: Inspired designs from the Netherlands, Japan, and beyond for resilient infrastructure.
  • Transparent Dashboard: Visible to DPWH, COA, LGUs, and the public.
  • M4GG (Mayors for Good Governance): Enables local oversight and direct citizen engagement.

By combining advanced technology with public participation, CorruptShield ensures that every peso works—and every person is empowered to watch its use.
