Welcome

CorruptShield is an AI-powered initiative to promote transparency, fight corruption, and improve governance in the Philippines. By combining open data, blockchain technology, and community participation, we aim to protect public funds and ensure infrastructure projects truly benefit the people.

Problem Statement

Every year, billions of pesos are lost to corruption in public infrastructure projects. For example, the Commission on Audit (COA) has repeatedly flagged the Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH) for irregularities such as ghost projects, substandard construction, and overpriced contracts. These anomalies not only waste taxpayer money but also result in unsafe roads, bridges, and flood control systems â€” putting Filipino communities at risk.

Our Solution

CorruptShield leverages AI for real-time anomaly detection in infrastructure data and blockchain for transparent, tamper-proof project records. Through public dashboards, integration with COA audits, and community-driven reporting, we make corruption harder to hide and accountability the standard. Our goal: ensure that every peso intended for development is used to build safe, sustainable, and resilient infrastructure for the people.
