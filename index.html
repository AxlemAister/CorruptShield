The Problem

For decades, the Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH) has been plagued with corruption scandals costing the Filipino people trillions of pesos. Key issues include:

  • ₱118.5 billion lost annually to infrastructure-related corruption (COA reports).
  • ₱1.9 trillion spent on flood control since 2010, with over ₱1 trillion allegedly lost to anomalies—yet floods persist nationwide.
  • Overpriced “cat’s eyes” road reflectors and rock netting projects, costing billions in padded contracts.
  • Bridges collapsed soon after completion due to substandard materials and labor.
  • Ghost projects, and “generic flood control” project names with no clear scope.
  • Collusion in bidding among contractors and unregulated bicameral insertions that bloat project costs.
  • Misuse of the Engineering Administrative Overhead (EAO) fund and lack of accountability.
  • During Senate hearings, DPWH officials, contractors, and agencies deflect responsibility—no one admits oversight.

Our Solution

CorruptShield introduces a transparent, AI-powered, and blockchain-backed platform to tackle these issues at their root:

  • AI Bid Vetting: Flags collusion, overpriced bids, and ghost contractors.
  • Blockchain Paper Trail: Immutable tracking of budgets, contracts, and milestones—AI can instantly answer querries regarding the projects.
  • LIDAR & Geospatial Verification: Confirms whether a project truly exists on the ground.
  • AI Categorization & Phase Tracking: Detects vague “generic projects,” assigns proper categories (dike, pumping station, sloping), and identifies progress stage.
  • Citizen Chatbot Interface: Communities can check status, report anomalies, and monitor spending in real time.
  • International Standards: Adapts best practices from Japan, the Netherlands, and Singapore in flood control and infrastructure governance.
  • Transparent Dashboard: Public, DPWH, COA, and LGUs see the same live data.
  • M4GG (Mayors for Good Governance): Local governments gain oversight and accountability tools.

By combining advanced technology, global standards, and citizen participation, CorruptShield ensures every peso works—and every project stands strong.
